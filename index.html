Sven-Erik Ekström

I am a tenure track assistant professor (biträdande universitetslektor/associate senior lecturer), since February 2021, at the Division of Scientific Computing, Department of Information Technology, Uppsala University, Uppsala, Sweden.

I defended my Ph.D. in scientific computing (numerical analysis) at Uppsala University in May 2018 with the thesis "Matrix-Less Methods for Computing Eigenvalues of Large Structured Matrices" (full text). Then, I was a postdoc at Athens University of Economics and Business and Bergische Universität Wuppertal, before receiving the International postdoc grant (Spring Call 2019, 3.15 MSEK ≈ 340 kUSD) of the Swedish Research Council.
